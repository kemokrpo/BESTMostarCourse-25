  Mostar, Bosnia   and Herzegovina

Mostar is a charming city in southern Bosnia and Herzegovina, famous for its stunning Old Bridge, known as Stari Most. The bridge is a symbol of the city's resilience and has become a significant tourist attraction, drawing visitors from around the world. The old town area around the bridge is also well-preserved and offers a glimpse into Mostar's historical and cultural significance. The city's old town is a delightful mix of Ottoman and Austro-Hungarian architecture, creating a unique atmosphere. You can explore the historic streets, visit the local bazaar for traditional crafts, and enjoy the peaceful Neretva River, which flows through the heart of the city. Mostar's rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality make it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

  About BEST

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a non-profit and non-political student organization. BEST aims to provide communication, cooperation, and exchange opportunities among European students of technology, thereby fostering international understanding and personal development. The organization offers a range of services, including academic courses, career support, and cultural exchange programs. One of its most popular activities is the organization of international engineering competitions, where students from different countries collaborate and compete in various technical challenges. Additionally, BEST organizes workshops, seminars, and other events that promote personal development and encourage the acquisition of new skills and knowledge.
